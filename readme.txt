Screencast.com Video Embedder is a plugin for Screencast.com users.

== Description ==

Screencast.com Video Embedder is a plugin that allows Screencast.com users an easy way to embed their content into their WordPress Blog or Website. Just enter the shortcode [screencast url="SCREENCAST SHORT URL" width="WIDTH" height="HEIGHT"] into your post or page and let the plugin do the rest! 

== Installation ==

1. Upload the Screencast folder to your /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Add the widget to your sidebar from Appearance->Widgets and configure the widget options.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can you add multiple videos into a post? =

Yes! Just add a shortcode for each video you want in your post.

= Do you have to enter a width and height? =

No. They are optional, however your video will not scale to fit within the post content area.
